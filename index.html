About Us

We brought an idea that the relationship between tattoo artists and their clients can be easy and agile, users can search and be interested in work and art by tattoo artists through this social network. Budgets can be made through the application, you describe what you want, where you want the size you want.

According to research by the German institute Dalia in 2019, the countries where there are the most tattoos follow the descending order of the following list: Italy, Sweden, USA, Argentina, Australia, Spain and Denmark between 40 to 50% of the population and United Kingdom, Brazil and France over 30% of the population. And worldwide the percentage of tattooed people is 38%.

The tattoo market grows around 25% a year, and already has more than 150,000 tattoo studios. 25% of people get tattooed only once and 75% have two or more. People between 30 and 49 years old represent 45% of tattooed people in the world and even during the Covid-19 pandemic, the market did not retreat.
